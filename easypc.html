Why Us

  • I have over 6 years of experience building computers, and I have been working with computers for my whole life.


  • Computers bought in stores are convenient; however, they are built with very low quality components, no upgradability in mind, and they cost sooooooo much more!


  • Store-bought computers cut corners. For example, many prebuilt computers will use 1 stick of 16GB RAM, when in reality the computer will be much faster with 2 sticks of 8GB of RAM.


  • Store-built computer companies like to misrepresent their computers by labeling them as Gaming Computers when they're really not. We make it easy to get a computer that works for the specific games you want so you don't overspend!


  • We care about transparency! It's hard to justify a price of a computer when you have no information. We make sure we provide you with the exact prices of each part so you know why your computer costs what it does!


  • We make custom PCs! Want a specific part in your computer? Ask! Want your computer to look a certain way? Ask! Want a certain brand of components? Ask!
